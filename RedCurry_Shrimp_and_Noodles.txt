Ingredients
1 tbsp. oil
1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
2 tbsp. red curry paste
1 c. coconut milk
2 tsp. fish sauce
12 oz. green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch lengths
12 oz. shelled, deveined shrimp
1/2 c. packed fresh basil leaves, plus more for garnish
8 oz. wide rice noodles, cooked
Lime wedges, for serving

Directions
In 12-inch skillet, heat oil on medium-high. Add red onion; cook 5 minutes, stirring. Add red curry paste; cook 1 minute, stirring. Whisk in coconut milk and fish sauce. Stir in green beans.
Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, covered, 8 minutes or until green beans are tender, stirring twice.
Uncover; stir in shrimp and basil leaves. Cook 3 minutes or until shrimp just turn opaque, stirring often. Toss wide rice noodles with curry. Serve with lime wedges and additional basil.

Nutrition Information (per serving): About 460 calories, 19 g protein, 59 g carbs, 17 g fat (11 g saturated fat), 5 g fiber, 1,305 mg sodium.
